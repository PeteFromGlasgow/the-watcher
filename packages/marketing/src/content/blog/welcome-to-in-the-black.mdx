We are thrilled to announce **In The Black**, a new open-source personal finance application designed from the ground up for people who take their money seriously.

## Why Another Budgeting App?

Most budgeting tools fall into two camps: oversimplified apps that lack real accounting rigour, or enterprise-grade software that is overkill for personal use. We wanted something in between — a tool that embraces **double-entry bookkeeping** without requiring an accounting degree to use.

## What Makes In The Black Different?

### Zero-Sum Budgeting
Every pound has a job. In The Black uses a zero-sum approach where your income is allocated across budget "pots" until there is nothing left unassigned. This ensures you always know exactly where your money is going.

### Open Banking Integration
Connect your UK bank accounts directly via Open Banking APIs. Transactions flow in automatically — no manual CSV imports or screen scraping.

### Self-Hostable
Your financial data is yours. Run In The Black on your own infrastructure with our Kubernetes-native architecture, or use our hosted offering when it launches.

### Built for Developers
- Full REST API for automation
- CLI tool for terminal workflows
- Custom query language for powerful filtering
- Extensible plugin architecture

## What's Next?

We are in active development and working towards our first public release. Here is what is on the roadmap:

1. **Multi-currency support** with live exchange rates
2. **AI-powered categorisation** for automatic transaction tagging
3. **Shared budgets** for households and teams
4. **Mobile app** for on-the-go tracking
